## Abstract

This talk introduces the ancient origins of problems that are still relevant today (key exchange problem, Kerchkoffs' principle, etc), building up to the modern notion of _foundations of cryptography_. The target is a beginner but interested audience.

## Notes

This talk was held 
- at [_PhD Day 2025_](https://sites.google.com/view/phdday-unito-polito-2025/home?authuser=1), organised by **Università di Torino** and **Politecnico di Torino**.
- at [_Collegio Bernardo Clesio_](https://eventi.unitn.it/it/aperitif-modern-cryptography), University of Trento, with a more lenient approach to theory.
